About

Bio

Hi, I'm la-bu — a developer interested in building clean, fast, and thoughtful software. I care about good design, clear writing, and making things that are a pleasure to use.

I work mostly in TypeScript and Python, and I'm drawn to the web as a platform because of how accessible it is to both builders and users.

Currently focused on

  • Building in public — writing about what I learn as I learn it
  • Web performance and the craft of minimal interfaces
  • Exploring static site generation and content-first web development

A personal note

Outside of code, I spend time reading, thinking about systems and ideas, and occasionally making things with my hands. This site is a place to think out loud — equal parts dev blog and digital notebook.
